The Somarathna Family Legacy

ALM SK Ceylon Cashews (Pvt) Ltd was born from the Somarathna family's deep-rooted passion for Sri Lankan cashew farming. The name ALM proudly represents the family's children, who now carry forward a proud legacy of quality and trust.

What began as a humble family operation has grown into a business built on three unshakeable pillars — Tradition, Trust, and Quality. Every cashew we produce reflects our commitment to authenticity and the pure taste of Ceylon.

Today, we serve both local communities and international buyers with the same care and dedication that has defined our family for generations.

Raw Cashews

Whole, unprocessed cashews naturally dried and preserved to retain maximum freshness, nutrition, and authentic Ceylon flavour.

  • Premium packaging
  • Retail & bulk available
