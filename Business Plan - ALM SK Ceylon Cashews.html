1. Executive Summary

ALM SK Ceylon Cashews (Pvt) Ltd is a family-owned cashew processing and trading business established in 2026, located in Diulwewa, Anamaduwa, Kurunegala, Sri Lanka. The business is founded by the Somarathna family with a deep-rooted passion for Sri Lankan cashew farming and a commitment to delivering premium quality cashews to both local and international markets.


The business is operated as a family partnership by three siblings — Lakruwan Somarathna, Anupama Somarathna, and Manoja Somarathna — who collectively manage and grow the business. The loan application is submitted by Mrs. Anupama Somarathna as the primary applicant, supported by her employment income, while all three partners are equally committed to the success of the business.


The company processes and sells three main product lines — Raw Cashews, Roasted Cashews, and Flavored Cashews — targeting retail consumers, bulk buyers, and export markets.

2.2 Business Description

ALM SK Ceylon Cashews sources raw cashews from local farmers across Sri Lanka, processes them into premium quality products, and distributes them through retail, bulk, and export channels. The business name ALM represents the Somarathna family's children, symbolizing a legacy of quality and trust passed down through generations.


The company operates on three core values: Tradition, Trust, and Quality — ensuring every product meets the highest standards of taste and freshness.
